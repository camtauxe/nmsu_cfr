Sources of Salary Saving

This page allows you to specify the sources of salary savings for your department. The table below will show the current most recent version of your department's savings. You can edit the values in the table or press the '+' to add a new entry. To remove an entry, check the checkbox in the last column. When you are done making your changes, press 'Update'.
